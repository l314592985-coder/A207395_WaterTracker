SDG 6: Clean Water and Sanitation

Goal

SDG 6 aims to ensure the availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all. The goal focuses on improving water efficiency, reducing unnecessary water waste, and promoting responsible water usage practices.

Why It Matters

Water is essential for human health, environmental sustainability and economic development. Many people are unaware of their daily water consumption habits, which can result in unnecessary waste and inefficient resource management.

How Water Usage Tracker Supports SDG 6

Water Usage Tracker helps users monitor daily water consumption, analyse usage trends, receive personalised recommendations, compare water usage statistics, and participate in community sharing activities. These features encourage sustainable water management and support the objectives of SDG 6.
